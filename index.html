My technical portfolio includes significant experience in managing and delivering large-scale microservice architecture projects, often utilizing Go for high-performance and scalability. I actively share my knowledge by giving workshops and training on modern technologies. I have successfully managed and contributed to projects across diverse sectors, including Super Apps, VOD Platforms, E-commerce, and Generative AI platforms.
